About Petabyt

Petabyt is a universal electronic health record for your animals. We are passionate about healthcare (both animal & human) and making it easier to keep your pets healthy through information technology.

Through Petabyt, we set out to solve a problem that we as animal custodians faced: to ensure that out animal's information was transmitted, not transported, available to those involved in their care, whenever and wherever it was needed.

CAPTURE IT ONCE - USE IT AGAIN AND AGAIN. AND NEVER LEAVE YOUR ANIMAL’S INFO STRANDED AT THE VET’S OR IN YOUR FILE CABINET.


How it works

First, give your household or organization a name.

Next, add your animals.

Next, add those that help you manage the household or organization.

Finally, start using Petabyt.
